## Monthly PR Cadence

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Week 1 | Check HARO/Qwoted daily; respond to 2-3 relevant queries |
| Week 2 | Draft and distribute press release (PRLog/OpenPR) |
| Week 3 | Follow up with 3 journalists from target list |
| Week 4 | Monthly roundup post on LinkedIn; review PR metrics |

## Preparation

- [ ] Prepare a 1-page media kit (company overview, key stats, founder bio, logo, headshots)
- [ ] Prepare 3 expert quotes on trending AI topics (ready to deploy for HARO responses)
- [ ] Build a list of 10 journalists covering AI/tech in Singapore
- [ ] Follow them on LinkedIn and engage with their posts
- [ ] Set up Google Alerts for "AI Singapore", "SME AI adoption", "PSG grant"
