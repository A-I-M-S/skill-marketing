# Directory Listings Checklist — Layer 2/3

## Why

Directories provide:
- Free backlinks for SEO (Layer 2)
- Entity validation for AI crawlers (Layer 3)
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) citations

## Global Directories

See `templates/directory-listings/global-listings.md` for the full list.

**Standard fields to prepare** (use the same text everywhere for consistency):
- Company name: {{COMPANY_NAME}}
- Website: https://{{COMPANY_DOMAIN}}
- Tagline: {{COMPANY_TAGLINE}}
- Description: 150-200 word description of your AI transformation services
- Categories: AI Consulting, Business Transformation, IT Strategy
- Location: Singapore
- Social links: LinkedIn, Twitter/X
- Logo: prepare a square logo (500x500px min)

## Singapore Directories

See `templates/directory-listings/sg-listings.md` for the full list.

**Priority:** These are more important than global ones for AI discoverability.
AI crawlers use Singapore-specific registries to validate entity existence.
