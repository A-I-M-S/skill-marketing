# Singapore Grants Reference — AI Transformation

Use in content, outreach, and lead qualification.

---

## PSG — Productivity Solutions Grant

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **What** | Co-funds adoption of IT solutions and equipment for business process improvement. AI solutions are eligible. |
| **Who** | Singapore-registered SMEs with ≥30% local shareholding. |
| **Coverage** | Up to 50% of qualifying costs. |
| **Cap** | $30,000 per project. |
| **Pre-approval required** | Yes. |
| **AI applicability** | Chatbots, automation, CRM/ERP with AI features, data analytics. |

---

## EDG — Enterprise Development Grant

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **What** | Supports business transformation projects including innovation, capability development, and market access. |
| **Who** | Singapore-registered companies. |
| **Coverage** | Up to 70% (SMEs) or up to 50% (non-SMEs). |
| **Cap** | $300,000 per project. |
| **Pre-approval required** | Yes. |
| **AI applicability** | AI strategy consulting, process re-engineering, custom AI solution development. |

---

## SkillsFuture Enterprise Credit (SFEC)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **What** | One-off $10,000 credit for business transformation and workforce upskilling. |
| **Who** | Singapore-registered employers. |
| **Coverage** | Up to 90% of qualifying costs. |
| **AI applicability** | AI training for staff, consultancy for transformation planning. |

---

## Champions of AI Programme

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **What** | National programme by AI Singapore to drive enterprise AI adoption. |
| **Who** | Singapore companies committed to deploying AI. |
| **AI applicability** | Expert networking, co-innovation, capability building. |
