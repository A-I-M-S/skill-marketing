# Buyer Persona — CTO / Head of Digital (Singapore SME)

## Demographics
- **Title:** CTO, Head of Digital, Head of Innovation, CDO, VP of Engineering
- **Company size:** 50–500 employees
- **Industry:** Any B2B / B2C — financial services, logistics, retail, healthcare, professional services
- **Location:** Singapore (HQ or regional hub)

## Pain Points
- Manual, repetitive processes consuming team time and causing operational errors.
- Pressure from CEO/board to "do something with AI" beyond simple chat helpers.
- Unsure where to start with **Agentic AI**—fears of agents "going rogue", loop errors, or API cost runway.
- Limited internal technical capability to orchestrate and deploy multi-agent architectures.
- Budget constraints—needs to show clear ROI and justify LLM token usage costs.
- Data privacy/security concerns regarding company knowledge bases processed by autonomous agents.

## Goals
- Reduce operational costs by 20–40% by substituting manual steps with autonomous workflows.
- Deploy reliable AI agents that can work 24/7 without constant human monitoring.
- Get a clear, prioritised roadmap for agentic business transformation.
- Find reliable implementation partners with Singapore-specific grant application experience (EDG/PSG).
- Leverage government grants to offset custom development costs.

## Information Sources
- LinkedIn (thought leadership, peer recommendations)
- Google / AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity)
- Technical forums & AI communities (Github, Hugging Face, Reddit)
- Industry events and meetups (SGTech, AI Singapore)
- Grant portals (IMDA, Enterprise Singapore)

## Common Objections
- "How do we monitor autonomous agents so they don't hallucinate or execute wrong actions?"
- "We tried basic chatbots before and they didn't work."
- "Our database infrastructure is too messy for agentic integration."
- "Custom multi-agent workflows are too expensive for our size."
- "Who takes responsibility if an agent makes a mistake in customer outreach?"

## What Converts Them
- Detailed case studies showing multi-agent systems performing complex, multi-step actions.
- A free **Agentic AI Opportunity Audit** (low friction, maps workflows directly to agentic roles).
- Pre-built templates showing prompt guardrails and agentic execution safety.
- Singapore grant co-funding awareness and application assistance.
