# Buyer Persona — CTO / Head of Digital (Singapore SME)

## Demographics
- **Title:** CTO, Head of Digital, Head of Innovation, CDO
- **Company size:** 50–500 employees
- **Industry:** Any B2B / B2C — financial services, logistics, retail, healthcare, professional services
- **Location:** Singapore (HQ or regional hub)

## Pain Points
- Manual processes consuming team time and causing errors
- Pressure from CEO/board to "do something with AI"
- Unsure where to start — tool overload, vendor noise
- Previous failed AI / digital transformation initiatives
- Limited internal technical capability for AI implementation
- Budget constraints — needs to show clear ROI

## Goals
- Reduce operational costs by 20–40%
- Automate repetitive workflows
- Get a clear, prioritised AI roadmap
- Find reliable implementation partners with Singapore experience
- Leverage government grants to offset cost

## Information Sources
- LinkedIn (thought leadership, peer recommendations)
- Google / AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity)
- Industry events and meetups (SGTech, AI Singapore)
- Grant portals (IMDA, Enterprise Singapore)
- G2 / Capterra for tool reviews

## Common Objections
- "We tried AI before and it didn't work"
- "We don't have the in-house expertise"
- "Our data isn't ready"
- "AI is too expensive for our size"
- "We need to see proof first"

## What Converts Them
- Industry-specific case studies with real numbers
- Free, low-friction assessment (no hard sell)
- Clear path from assessment → implementation → results
- Grant co-funding awareness and assistance
- Peer recommendations and social proof
